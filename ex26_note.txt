You can check your answer to my answer easily on Unix like operating systems
like Linux (Ubuntu, Debian, macOS, etc.).

Type this at your command line (terminal), replace 'ex26_yours.py' with your
file:
diff ex26.py ex26_yours.py
or you can diff with the given file and my file to see what I fixed:
diff ex26.py exercise26.txt

You can learn more about diff by typing 'man diff' into your command line.
